About C.G.C.C.C.

 We act as a strategic connector of entrepreneurs, executives, institutions, and policymakers, creating cross-border trade opportunities, expanding professional networks, and facilitating impactful investment partnerships. CGCC–Calgary brings over 30 years of collective expertise in fostering public-private partnerships, regional integration, and diaspora-led development initiatives.

Our Vision

To be a catalyst for Africa–Canada economic integration and a bridge for shared prosperity between communities, institutions, and industries. The Chamber seeks to position Calgary and Alberta as leading gateways for trade and investment with Ghana and the wider West African region.

We envision CGCC–Calgary as a recognized hub of influence, advancing industrial policy, technology transfer, renewable energy investment, youth entrepreneurship, and infrastructure innovation between both nations.

Our Mission

To create an inclusive and transformative platform for professionals, entrepreneurs, and institutions—particularly from the Ghanaian Canadian and Alberta business communities—to collaborate, innovate, and lead economic growth in both Canada and Ghana. We are dedicated to supporting business development, international expansion, and strategic policy engagement through high-value programming and advocacy.

Business Facilitation & Operation Models

Our Chamber adopts multiple facilitation frameworks to accelerate international
business engagement and sustainable development. These include:

Market Entry Support
  • Trade & regulatory briefings
  • Local partner sourcing & due diligence
  • Business incorporation assistance
  • Legal, tax, and labor compliance support
Investment Pipeline Development
  • Deal origination and validation
  • Project profiling and pitch preparation
  • Capital access strategies
  • Coordination with investment authorities (e.g., GIPC, Invest Alberta)
Capacity Building and Knowledge Exchange
  • Executive education and certification programs
  • Innovation labs and incubation for startups
  • ESG (Environmental, Social, Governance) compliance training
  • Smart technology & green energy transformation support
Investment Pipeline Development
  • Skills and knowledge repatriation platforms
  • Think Tank initiatives
  • Youth entrepreneurship support
  • Diaspora Bond and community investment projects
Bilateral And Multilateral Engagement
  • Ministerial roundtables and industry consultations
  • Diaspora capital mobilizationCanada–Africa business forums
  • Participation in government missions
