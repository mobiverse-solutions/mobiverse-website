Why Join CGCC Calgary?

We are not only a Chamber, we are a strategic launchpad for global business success. Our leadership team offers cross-sectoral expertise, deep diplomatic relationships, and hands-on experience in deal facilitation, strategic policy engagement, and community development.
Whether you’re a company looking to expand to Ghana, a policymaker seeking partnerships, or a young entrepreneur building your global footprint, CGCC–Calgary is your trusted partner.

Who Can Join?

We welcome individuals and organizations from across the spectrum of public and private sectors including:

  • Entrepreneurs, SMEs, and corporate entities
  • Professionals, consultants, and trade advisors
  • Government agencies and public institutions
  • Investment firms, financiers, and export houses
  • Educational, research, and development institutions
  • Diaspora networks and youth-led enterprises
