Imagine a world where members contribute seamlessly, track their finances in real-time, and stay engaged effortlessly. That's exactly what we offer.

The Challenge

Social groups often struggle with fragmented communication and messy tracking of their financial contributions

The Transparency Crisis

Members often felt uncertain about their standing. "Did I pay for March?" became a recurring point of friction.

The "Nag" Fatigue

Executives had to manually send hundreds of individual WhatsApp messages and make phone calls to remind members to pay.

The Disengagement Fade

The group starts to feel like a financial chore rather than a thriving community.

The Solution

A central hub to manage members, dues, finances, and fundraising with ease.

Real-time Clarity

Upenza provides a transparent, self-service dashboard where members can view their payment history bridging the "Trust Gap."

Unified Accuracy (Manual + Digital)

Whether payments are made through the automated wallet or entered manually by the treasurer, Upenza instantly reconciles the data into a single source of truth for your group.

Effortless Collections

One-tap payment reminders via Email and WhatsApp handle the "nagging" for you, resulting in 90% faster collections.

Strengthened Connection

By removing the friction of financial administration, leadership can focus on the group's mission, turning a "financial chore" back into a thriving community.
