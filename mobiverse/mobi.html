The Challenge

Businesses across Africa need reliable, scalable SMS but face significant barriers to effective messaging.

Poor Delivery Rates

Many SMS providers deliver less than 70% of messages, meaning critical alerts and marketing campaigns never reach the customer.

Complex Integration

Existing SMS APIs are poorly documented and hard to integrate, costing developers days of work for basic functionality.

Unpredictable Pricing

Hidden fees, per-network surcharges, and opaque pricing models make budgeting for SMS campaigns nearly impossible.

The Solution

A powerful, developer-friendly SMS platform that makes bulk campaigns and transactional messaging simple, reliable, and instant.

Bulk SMS Campaigns

Send thousands of personalized messages with ease. Schedule campaigns, segment your audience, and track results.

Transactional Messaging via API

Seamlessly integrate secure SMS (like OTPs and order alerts) directly into your apps and workflows.

Real-Time Delivery Reports

Know exactly when and how your messages are delivered with detailed engagement tracking.

Secure & Scalable Infrastructure

Rest easy with a platform built for high performance, privacy, and reliability, handling millions of messages effortlessly.
