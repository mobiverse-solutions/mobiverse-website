Yelgu integrated AI-driven insights into support ecosystems, driving peak agent performance, while providing top-level management with a clear window into the customer voice.

The Challenge

Businesses were operating in a "data blackout," where manual oversight and a lack of real-time intelligence left supervisors overwhelmed, agents stagnant, and management disconnected from the customer reality.

Stagnant Agent Growth

Call centre agents lacked the clear insights needed to self-correct, refine their skills, and improve interaction quality.

Manual Quality Assurance

Supervisors could only manually review less than 1% of calls, leaving massive gaps in compliance and agent training.

Agent Burnout

Without real-time coaching, agents struggle with difficult calls, leading to high turnover and training costs.

The Solution

A central "intelligence layer" for engagement strategy, providing real-time guidance and deep analytics.

Smart Call Management

Provided a live, centralized view of call center health, allowing managers to reallocate resources dynamically based on peak demand.

AI-Powered Analytics

Leveraged machine learning to perform real-time sentiment analysis and automated tagging, identifying customer and agent pain points as they happen.

Advanced Monitoring Dashboards

Provided leadership with a live, centralized view of call center health and agent KPIs for data-driven decision-making.

Exclusive Private Hosting

Deployed the solution on secure, dedicated infrastructure to ensure maximum data privacy and high-speed performance for enterprise operations.

Proactive Support & Maintenance

Established a continuous technical oversight framework to ensure 99.9% uptime and seamless system optimizations.
