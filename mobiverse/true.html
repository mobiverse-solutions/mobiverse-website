The Strategy

Most tech is built for the best-case scenario. We build for the reality of the African landscape. Whether it's a feature phone in a remote village or a high-velocity call centre in the city, our tools ensure no one is left out of the digital economy.

The Challenge

Businesses struggle to connect with offline customers, face high costs for custom USSD development, or lack the technical resources to manage USSD services. Legacy authentication methods are failing businesses and frustrating customers across the continent.

The Connectivity Blind Spot

Apps and websites exclude millions who still use basic feature phones. Businesses focused only on "connected" customers are leaving the majority behind.

The Maintenance Nightmare

Updating a USSD menu means going back to the developer every time. Simple changes take days or weeks, killing agility.

The Cost Barrier

Custom USSD development is slow and expensive—requiring telecom expertise and ongoing developer support that most businesses can't afford.

The Solution

A central hub to manage members, dues, finances, and fundraising with ease.

The Brainwave

A drag-and-drop USSD platform that lets any business launch and manage interactive mobile services, no coding required.

Real-time Reach

UZO enables businesses to engage customers on any mobile phone, anywhere. No internet. No smartphone. Just a simple dial code and instant connection.

Unified Simplicity

Whether you need a farmer advisory line, a banking verification portal, or a hospital appointment checker, UZO handles it all from one intuitive dashboard.

Effortless Deployment

Launch USSD applications in days, not weeks. Drag, drop, and publish — without waiting on developers or telecom consultants.

Cost-Efficient Scale

Cut development costs by eliminating the need for specialized USSD programmers. Scale from 100 interactions a day to 1 million without re-architecting your solution.
