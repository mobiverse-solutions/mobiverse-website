The Challenge

Reaching offline customers shouldn't be a technical or financial hurdle. Most businesses face three major roadblocks when trying to scale with USSD.

I

Disconnected Offline Audiences

Many businesses struggle to engage customers in regions with limited internet access, losing touch with a massive segment of their target market.

II

Technical Integration Complexity

Traditional USSD development is slow and resource-heavy, often turning into a maintenance nightmare that drains your engineering team's time.

III

Prohibitive Entry Costs

High setup fees and opaque pricing models often make USSD feel like an inaccessible luxury for growing enterprises.

The Solution

A drag-and-drop USSD platform that lets any business launch and manage interactive mobile services, no coding required.

Real-time Reach

UZO enables businesses to engage customers on any mobile phone, anywhere. No internet. No smartphone. Just a simple dial code and instant connection.

Self-Service USSD Provisioning

Eliminated the traditional telco waiting period by providing an instant-access portal for acquiring and managing dedicated or shared short codes with zero technical friction.

Effortless Deployment

Launch USSD applications in days, not weeks. Drag, drop, and publish—without waiting on developers or telecom consultants.

Cost-Efficient Scale

Cut development costs by eliminating the need for specialized USSD programmers. Scale from 100 interactions a day to 1 million without re-architecting your solution.
